Keitumetse Mokwena: Bridging Education and Technology
I am Keitumetse Mokwena, a South African hailing from Hammanskraal. I am a trailblazing young teacher with a passion for both education and technology. My journey is one of determination, curiosity, and a desire to make a difference. As a math and NSTech educator, I realized the immense potential technology holds for transforming education. I believe that every student deserves access to quality learning experiences, regardless of their background. Venturing into Tech, driven by my curiosity and a desire to empower my students, I decided to venture into the tech world. I saw firsthand how technology could enhance learning, engage students, and open doors to new opportunities. With unwavering determination, I embarked on a path to bridge the gap between education and technology.
